Bruce Lee

Actor and inventor of Jeet Kune Do

Bruce Lee's epic timeline:

  • 1940 - Born in Chinatown, San Francisco.
  • 1956 - Begins Wing Chun training with Yip Man.
  • 1958 - Wins Kong's Crown Colony Cha-Cha Championship.
  • 1961 - Enrolls at the University of Washington to study dramatic arts, philosophy, psychology, among others.
  • 1964 - Drops out of college and moves to Oakland. Founds the 2nd Jun Fan martial arts studio in town.
  • 1966-1970 - Plays the role of Kato in The Green Hornet.
  • 1967 - Creates Jeet Kune Do (a martial arts discipline).
  • 1971 - Plays his first leading role in The Big Boss.
  • 1972 - Plays lead role in Enter the Dragon.
  • 1973 - Dies in Hong Kong at the age of 32.
